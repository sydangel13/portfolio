Hey there, I'm Sydney!

I am a second-year Master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information studying User Experience Research and Human Computer Interaction.

I previously graduated from the University of Michigan, where I double-majored in Biopsychology, Cognition and Neuroscience and Cognitive Science with a focus in decision-making.

I am incredibly passionate about creating digital content that is easily accessible to all types of users, through empathetic design solutions and data-driven results. Ultimately, I hope to plan and develop clear, relevant content that aligns with the users' needs, as well as the business' goals. I strive to accomplish this through fleshed-out research and analysis.

In my free time I enjoy playing with my six month old golden-doodle, watching Broadway musicals, hiking through national parks and listening to True Crime podcasts.
