About Sydney

I am a first year Master's student at the University of Michigan studying User Experience Research and Design with a primary focus in research and content strategy.

I am incredibly passionate about creating digital content that is easily accessible to all types of users, through empathetic design solutions and personal adaptability. Ultimately, I hope to plan and develop clear, relevant content that aligns with the user's needs, as well as the business goals. I strive to accomplish this through flushed-out research and analysis.

I previously graduated from the University of Michigan, where I double-majored in Biopsychology, Cognition and Neuroscience and Cognitive Science with a focus in decision-making. In my free time I enjoy playing with my six month old golden-doodle, watching Broadway musicals, hiking through national parks and listening to True Crime podcasts.
