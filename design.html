Weather Banner Design

Project Overview

The purpose of this assignment was to create six minimalistic weather screens using a combination of Photoshop and Illustrator.

In order to create these designs, I used various combinations of my own created shapes, and images curated from reusable sources.

Color was implemented in this design to give the user a sense of time of day, weather and general location.

Project Process

Before designing the weather states, I first did some preliminary research of current minimalistic weather apps to see what they looked like. After getting a good idea of what the competitors were doing, I create several sketches covering different iterations of what I wanted the application to look like.

Once I created a design I was happy with, I started wireframing and creating a barebones example of what the designs looked like in my head.

Once the wireframe was done, I decided to create color palettes using the online tool Adobe Color.

After selecting the colors I would use for the design, I began creating each screen.
